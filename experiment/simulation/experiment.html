UTM Machine

Vernier Caliper

Test Sample

Step 1: Take length of the sample
  • Drag the sample and venier caliper from menu to the canvas. and take the length of the sample.
  • Right click on venier caliper and click on pin.
  • Place the sample on the venier caliper.
  • Take the length and enter it below:
Length: mm
Step 2: Take the diameter of the sample
  • Right click on venier caliper and click on unload sample.
  • Now, Right click on sample and click on rotate.
  • Place the sample on the venier caliper.
  • Take the diameter and enter it below:
Diameter: mm
Step 3: Place sample on UTM machine.
  • Unload the sample from venier caliper.
  • Remove the venier caliper by clicking on delete.
  • Take the UTM machine from menu.
  • Place the sample on the machine.
  • You can adjust zoom level also.
Step 4: Start the test.
  • Click below buttons to start the machine.
Load in kN Extensometer Reading in Division Ivory Scale Reading(mm)
A B Average
Step 5: Measure final length.
  • Unload the sample from UTM.
  • Remove the UTM by clicking on delete.
  • Take the venier caliper from menu.
  • Right click on venier caliper and click on "Pin"
  • Place the sample on the venier caliper.
  • Take the length of the sample and enter it below.
Length: mm
Step 6: Measure final diameter
  • Right click on venier caliper and click on unload sample.
  • Now, Right click on sample and click on rotate.
  • Place the sample on the venier caliper.
  • Take the diameter and enter it below:
Diameter: mm
